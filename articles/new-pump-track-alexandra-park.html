Proposals made for new pump track at Alexandra Park


22ND NOV 2025

CramlingtonNews Editors

Cramlington Town Council have submitted plans for a new pump track at Alexandra Park - a tarmac track with ramps and curves for scooters, mountain bikes and BMX bikes.

The Town Council have announced plans to redevelop the current seating area in the Alexandra Park activity zone, with a new tarmac track formed. They are encouraging people from the surrounding areas to share their views on the plans to make it work for everyone.

A statement was released on their Facebook page yesterday afternoon explaining the benefits of the track and the postitve health benefits it gives. Here is what they said: "A pump track is a specially designed circuit of ramps and turns ridden by “pumping” your bike or scooter to gain momentum. It's suitable for beginners and experienced riders alike, including BMX and mountain bike users.

Pump tracks elsewhere in the UK have become popular community spaces, supporting everything from school group activities to organised events, and they're known to bring social, physical, and mental health benefits."
