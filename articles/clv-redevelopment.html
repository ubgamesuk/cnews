Cramlington Learning Village redevelopment plans - from the classrooms to the social areas



23RD OCT 2024

CramlingtonNews Editors


Many years after the original announcement, the official plans for the rebuilding of Cramlington Learning Village were released on the 22nd October 2024 - over two years after the school was officially chosen for the school rebuilding programme.

Pictured below are the proposed interior and exterior designs of the new complex. Construction is expected to start in Summer next year and due to be completed in 2027.
