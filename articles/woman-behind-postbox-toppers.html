Louise Bryant: the woman behind the amazing crochet postbox toppers around Cramlington


6TH AUG 2025

CramlingtonNews Editors, BBC

Cramlington resident Louise Bryant has been brightening peoples' days by creating colourful postbox toppers to put in locations across the North East.

Louise started to make the toppers a few years back, and her creations have now become a popular sight across the North East. The toppers often resemble a famous figure or something relating to a current event - such as a tribute to the late Ozzy Osbourne (pictured above next to Louise).

Her other creations include Harry Kane for the 2024 Euros, a magpie for Newcastle United placed in Newcastle city centre and Elvis Presley, to name a few.

Louise plans to continue creating even more crochet toppers in the coming months and to display on postboxes in locations around the North East. Watch the video to find out about her work!
